The team behind the work.

Architects and engineers who like solving difficult problems on facilities that have to keep running. Many of us have spent our careers in healthcare and federal work; all of us share a commitment to doing the job right.

Founder
Don Maggioli, P.E.

Don founded Alares in 2007 after a career that began at the United States Military Academy at West Point and continued as an officer of the elite Green Berets. The leadership training and operational standards he learned in that earlier career are the same ones he applies to every project — clarity of mission, rigor in execution, and accountability to outcomes, not just intent.

Today, he leads the firm's federal and healthcare practice, focusing on complex VA hospital infrastructure, central utility plants and seismic compliance work. He's a licensed Professional Engineer and a hands-on technical leader on the firm's most demanding projects.

Working with us

We're a small enough firm that the people you meet at the kickoff are the people who do the work. There's no hand-off from a senior pitch team to a junior delivery team. Every project has a director-level engineer involved from concept through closeout.

For federal and healthcare clients, that direct accountability is the difference between a project that ships and a project that drifts. It's the reason our clients keep coming back.
