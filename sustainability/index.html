Capabilities / 01 — 05

Five disciplines that compound into real savings.

01
Energy auditing

Energy auditing is the foundation of any energy-efficiency plan. Comprehensive audits to identify inefficiencies and opportunities for cost savings.

  • Detailed analysis of HVAC, lighting and equipment energy consumption
  • Identification of key energy-saving measures with ROI analysis
  • Compliance with ASHRAE Levels I, II and III audits
  • Recent: audited 80M+ sq ft for VA hospitals — identified $2M+ in annual savings
02
Geothermal feasibility & design

A proven, sustainable way to reduce energy costs and reliance on fossil fuels.

  • Geological site analysis to determine geothermal potential
  • Feasibility studies assessing ROI, cost savings and efficiency
  • Complete system design — ground loops, heat pumps, distribution
  • Recent: Connecticut National Guard system projected to cut heating and cooling costs 30%
03
Advanced metering

Real-time data on energy consumption, empowering facility managers to make informed decisions.

  • Energy meters and submeters by zone or system
  • Real-time monitoring of electricity, gas and water
  • BMS integration with custom dashboards
  • Recent: 60 federal buildings in New England — 15% reduction in energy waste in year one
04
Energy modeling

Predict a building's consumption, optimizing design before construction begins.

  • Performance simulation with eQUEST and EnergyPlus
  • Assessment of envelope, glazing and HVAC tradeoffs
  • LEED certification support through validated savings
  • Recent: 200,000-sq-ft VA medical facility — design adjustments saved $300K
05
Net-zero design

Net-zero buildings balance energy consumption with energy production. We specialize in designing and retrofitting facilities to get there.

  • Integration of on-site renewable energy systems like solar PV and wind
  • Energy-efficient systems and passive design strategies
  • Comprehensive auditing and modeling to plan for net-zero performance
  • Recent: net-zero building for the GSA in Fitchburg, Massachusetts
