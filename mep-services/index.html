Capabilities / 01 — 03

Three disciplines, one coordinated team.

01
Mechanical engineering

Efficient, sustainable systems that ensure comfort, reliability and optimal energy performance.

  • HVAC design and optimization (air handlers, chillers, boilers)
  • Central Utility Plant design and upgrades
  • Steam and condensate piping systems
  • Energy modeling and system performance analysis
  • Sustainable mechanical solutions aligned with LEED and federal energy standards
02
Electrical engineering

Power your facilities with safe, reliable and energy-efficient solutions.

  • Power distribution systems, transformers and switchgear upgrades
  • Emergency and backup power systems, including generators
  • Lighting design for interior and exterior applications
  • Renewable energy integration (solar PV and Combined Heat & Power)
  • Electrical load analysis and energy management systems
03
Plumbing engineering

Innovative plumbing designs to ensure safe, functional and efficient water systems.

  • Domestic hot and cold water systems
  • Sanitary and storm drainage systems
  • Medical gas systems for healthcare facilities
  • High-efficiency plumbing systems for water conservation
