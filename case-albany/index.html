Case study / 01

Campus boiler plant replacement & electric upgrades.

Alares served as the prime designer for the "Design – Replace Boilers" project at the Albany VAMC. The project exemplifies our expertise in MEP and structural design while emphasizing our deep understanding of VA design guidelines.

← All case studies
Client

Department of Veterans Affairs

Location

Albany VAMC, NY

Disciplines

MEP · Structural · Civil

Role

Prime designer

Detail

Overview

The project involved replacing aging boilers, upgrading critical utility infrastructure and ensuring compliance with local and federal VA requirements. Our team provided a comprehensive suite of design services, including:

  • Preliminary concepts and layouts
  • Program development
  • Investigative services
  • Preparation of contract drawings and specifications
  • Cost estimating services

Scope and challenges

Existing systems investigation

  • Detailed assessment of steam and condensate piping and boiler plant equipment
  • Upgrades to campus switchgear for the Central Utility Plant and transformers
  • Integration of a new backup generator for the boiler plant

VA design guideline adherence

  • Coordination with the Albany VA engineering team for full compliance
  • Resolution of issues outside the original scope (e.g. boiler plant chimney stack to meet air quality standards) — without requiring a change order

Innovative solutions

  • 3D Revit modeling. Comprehensive model of the existing boiler plant and all piping — significantly improved the quality and clarity of design documents.
  • Proactive collaboration. Frequent engagement with the Albany VA engineering team prevented delays and cost overruns.
  • Advanced design. New piping designed to replace the existing system with full engineering calculations and precise construction estimates.

Key outcomes

  • Cost efficiency. Resolving issues like the non-compliant chimney stack during design avoided costly change orders.
  • High-quality documentation. The 3D Revit model reduced miscommunication and minimized errors during construction.
  • Compliance and coordination. Project adhered to all VA design guidelines and federal requirements.
  • Enhanced infrastructure. The new boiler plant design ensures long-term reliability, safety and efficiency.
