About the team

We're a team of architects and engineers who like solving difficult problems on facilities that have to keep running, VA hospitals, federal campuses, healthcare and commercial buildings.

Most of our work is technical, mission-critical and federal-grade. If you've worked on healthcare or government projects before, you'll feel at home. If you haven't, we'll teach you. What we look for is curiosity, rigor and a genuine interest in doing the job right.

We hire architects, MEP engineers, structural engineers, commissioning agents and project managers across both offices. Open to early-career and senior-level candidates.
